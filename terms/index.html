05 Reflections System

Vespera uses a unit-based system called Reflections to grant access to spiritual content categories. Understanding how this system works is essential to using the App.

How Reflections Work

  • Each time you select and access a content category (e.g. Morning Prayer, Evening Vespers, Saint of the Day), one (1) Reflection is deducted from your balance.
  • Reflections are consumed at the point of category access, regardless of how much time you spend with the resulting content.
  • Reflections are account-bound, non-transferable, and have no cash or monetary value.
  • Reflections cannot be exchanged, refunded, or converted into any other currency, credit, or item of value.

Acquiring Reflections

  • The App includes 150 Reflections upon purchase, credited to your Account when the App is first launched.
  • Additional Reflections may be acquired as top-ups through in-app purchases processed via the Platform Store.
  • Purchasing the Blessed tier adds 300 Reflections to your Account balance at the time of purchase.
  • Promotional Reflections may be issued at our discretion and may carry specific terms, expiration dates, or usage restrictions.

Expiry & Balance

Reflections acquired through in-app purchase do not expire while your Account remains active. Promotional or complimentary Reflections may carry an expiry date, which will be communicated at the time of issuance.
